Understanding Mental Health

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

Warning Signs

  • • Feeling sad or withdrawn for more than 2 weeks
  • • Severe mood swings
  • • Intense worries or fears
  • • Changes in eating or sleeping
  • • Difficulty concentrating

You're Not Alone

1 in 5 adults experience mental health issues. Mental health conditions are treatable, and recovery is possible.

Self-Care Strategies

Physical Wellness

  • Regular exercise (even 10-15 minutes daily)
  • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • Eat nutritious meals regularly
  • Limit alcohol and avoid drugs
  • Stay hydrated

Mental Wellness

  • Practice mindfulness or meditation
  • Keep a gratitude journal
  • Set realistic goals
  • Learn stress management techniques
  • Engage in hobbies you enjoy

Social Wellness

  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Join support groups or communities
  • Volunteer for causes you care about
  • Practice saying no to overwhelming commitments
  • Seek professional help when needed

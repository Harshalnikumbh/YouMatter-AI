About Us

YouMatter is an AI-powered mental health detector designed to provide timely emotional support and raise awareness about mental well-being.Trained on 6,00,000+ real-world data entries, our model achieves an accuracy of 79%, making it a reliable tool to identify patterns of emotional struggles such as anxiety, depression, and loneliness.

In today’s fast-paced world, mental health struggles often remain unseen and unspoken.YouMatter provides a safe, private, and intelligent space where individuals can freely express their thoughts through text and receive meaningful insights into their emotional well-being. By combining empathy with technology, YouMatter aims to make emotional support more accessible, reliable, and stigma-free.

What It's Made to Do

Text-Based Emotional Screening

Users enter what they're feeling in a text box. Our system analyzes the language using natural language processing to detect emotional patterns.

Mental Health Signal Detection

YouMatter uses machine learning to detect signs such as Anxiety, Depression, or when things are Normal.

Instant and Private Feedback

The system provides a private, non-judgmental reflection on the user's input, offering reassurance or suggestions to encourage awareness and self-care.

Promoting Early Awareness

YouMatter aims to reduce the stigma around mental health by enabling users to take the first step toward understanding their emotional state.
